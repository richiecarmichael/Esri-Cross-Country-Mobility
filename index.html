Cross Country Mobility

Welcome to the cross country mobility application designed and developed by Esri's applications prototype lab. The purpose of this application is to route the optimal path from one or more origins to a single destination in the United States for three modes of transport.

Please click Next below to select your route's start and end locations.

Position the origin of the cross country voyage. To add more than one origin click the add origin button in the lower left hand corner of the map.

Click Next to progress to begin processing or Previous to modify the destination.

The results of your analysis will be available momentarily.
